Introduction

Hi there, I'm Adam, but I typically go by the name "acal17" (or a variation thereof) online. The purpose of this website is to serve as a portfolio of sorts, to showcase my work and to provide a place for me to write about things that interest me. It also serves as a personal landing page from which my other pages can be found.

About me

I am currently an undergraduate at the University of Edinburgh, three years into my four-year Computer Science course. I am active in two societies: I am the Technical Secretary of CompSoc Edinburgh, the computing society, and I also participate in SIGINT, which focuses on cybersecurity. You will often find me competing in CTFs with the SIGINT team, where my preferred topics are reverse engineering and binary exploitation.
